## Style Prompt
Match the real Book Manage frontend running on localhost:5173: a light AI writing workspace with translucent cards, pale blue and warm paper gradients, teal primary actions, peach/amber accents, compact data cards, and quiet SaaS ergonomics. The video should feel like an enlarged, polished version of the actual app UI rather than a separate cinematic brand world.

## Colors
- Background: hsl(205 50% 97%), pale blue app background.
- Card: hsl(48 35% 99%), warm white cards and panels.
- Foreground: hsl(211 48% 13%), deep blue-black text.
- Muted: hsl(211 22% 42%), slate-blue secondary text.
- Primary: hsl(176 67% 24%), teal primary buttons and active states.
- Secondary: hsl(169 38% 91%), pale teal surfaces.
- Accent: hsl(28 95% 91%), peach highlight surfaces.
- Border: hsl(198 31% 84%), soft blue-gray card outlines.

## Typography
- Display/UI: "Noto Sans JP", sans-serif, 600-700 weight for Chinese headings.
- Interface/Data: "IBM Plex Mono", monospace, 400-600 weight for metrics and status labels.
- Body: "IBM Plex Sans Condensed", sans-serif, 350-600 weight for compact labels and descriptions.

## Motion Rules
- Primary transition: medium-energy horizontal push slide between app workspaces.
- Accent transition: focus-pull for AI analysis and final reveal.
- Entrances should feel like UI panels loading into place, with restrained movement and short stagger.
- Background decoratives should mimic the real app: thin diagonal lines, faint grid texture, teal/orange radial washes.

## What NOT to Do
- No dark cinematic manuscript theme.
- No oversized serif title-card typography.
- No neon or purple/blue product-promo gradients.
- No layouts that diverge from the real app's cards, buttons, borders, and compact workbench density.
- No jump cuts between scenes.
